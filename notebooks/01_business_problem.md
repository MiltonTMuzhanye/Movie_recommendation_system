# Business Problem: Movie Recommendation Engine

## Why Recommendations Matter
- Increase user engagement and retention
- Drive content discovery and consumption
- Improve user satisfaction and personalization
- Increase platform revenue through better engagement

## Key Challenges
1. **Cold-start problem**: New users/movies with no interaction history
2. **Scalability**: Handling millions of users and items
3. **Personalization**: Tailoring recommendations to individual preferences
4. **Freshness**: Keeping recommendations updated with new content

## Success Metrics
### Business Metrics
- Click-through Rate (CTR) on recommendations
- Conversion rate (watch/rent/buy)
- User retention and engagement time
- Revenue per user

### ML Metrics
- Precision@K, Recall@K
- Mean Average Precision (MAP)
- Root Mean Square Error (RMSE)
- Mean Absolute Error (MAE)

### Operational Metrics
- Recommendation latency (< 100ms)
- Model training time
- System uptime and reliability
